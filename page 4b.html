“Wow it’s been years since we went to an amusement park!” Ice Bear exclaimed.

“Hey it’s what brothers do! And please, stop saying that! You’re acting like you’re gonna die tomorrow! You still have a month left. We still have lots of time to spend together.” Panda Bear responded.

“Okay that’s enough for today. From now on, we’re gonna try our best to just live happily. Anyways, it’s been a long day, let’s get some sleep now. Goodnight Panpan and Icy.” Grizzly Bear said.
