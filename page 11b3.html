Ice Bear’s POV

Panpan never woke up. It’s been a year now. His brain got too damaged. We already took him off life support last week. It was really painful to sit there and watch him slowly die. We knew that we were just prolonging his agony by not letting him go.

I miraculously got healed. I’ve never appreciated life as much as I do now. Nothing in life is guaranteed and I learned that the hard way. Before Panpan died, I was always afraid to take risks. I was always too scared to fail. But Panpan was the opposite. He taught me that life is all about doing things we’re scared of.
